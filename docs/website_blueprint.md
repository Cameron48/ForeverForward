# Forever Forward Website Blueprint

## PDF Analysis

### Brand style guide

- Mission: help individuals build strength, discipline, and purpose through physical training and transformation.
- Brand values: resilience, discipline, purpose, leadership, transformation.
- Tone: confident, grounded, direct, disciplined, authentic.
- Visual system: matte black, steel gray, phoenix gold, phoenix ember, white.
- Aesthetic: cinematic, dark, minimalist, premium, mobile-first.

### Launch roadmap

- Primary website goals: consultation bookings, training sales, military prep sales, speaking inquiries.
- Main conversion: book a free consultation.
- Core navigation: Home, About, Training, Military Prep, Speaking, Book Consultation.
- Integrations: Stripe, Calendly, Google Calendar sync, optional email capture.
- Future expansion: online coaching, client portal, app integration, merch, video courses.

## Structural Decisions

- Build a static multi-page foundation first so content and hierarchy are easy to refine.
- Keep copy short and assertive to match "bold, succinct, aggressive but minimal."
- Make the consultation page the clearest conversion destination.
- Leave explicit embed zones for Stripe and Calendly instead of faking live integrations.

## Page Roles

- `index.html`: brand statement, transformation framing, service overview, primary CTA.
- `about.html`: story, values, trust, philosophy.
- `training.html`: service explanation, package structure, purchase path.
- `military-prep.html`: six-week prep offer and tactical audience fit.
- `speaking.html`: topic positioning and inquiry form.
- `book-consultation.html`: central scheduling destination.
